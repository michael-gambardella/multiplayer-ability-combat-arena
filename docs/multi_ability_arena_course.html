Answer: The team with fewer players

Answer: Assign a default hero

Week 3 Quiz

1. Which step happens before HP subtraction in damage flow?

Answer: Modifier and invulnerability checks

2. Assist credit should use:

Answer: Recent damage window

Week 4 Quiz

1. Cooldowns are best stored as:

Answer: Map(agent -> map(slot -> last used time))

2. Ultimate cooldown should begin:

Answer: After ultimate effect ends

Answer: Cone check using dot product

2. Specter invisibility should break when:

Answer: Dealing damage or timer expiry

Answer: Only negative tagged effects

2. Gravity Snare pull direction is:

Answer: Normalize(snare center - target position)

Week 7 Quiz

2. Tie at match end should trigger:

Answer: Sudden death

Week 8 Quiz

2. Cooldown icons should be:

Answer: Grayed on cooldown and glow when ready

Week 9 Quiz

Answer: Avoid positional advantage

Week 10 Quiz
